Lightworks

- A beat by J Dilla

Lightworks is an infous hip hop beat by the late Jay Dilla, one of the most influential hip hop producers of all time. His signature is pushing hard swung beats and meticulous sample chopping on his trusty MPC 3000 sampler.

The beat is a rework of electronic music pioneer Raymond Scott's song of the same title. Vocals and instruments are rearragned to create a funky retro robotic sound, interlaced with sirens off Mantronix's King Of The Beats and vocal chops off Skillz & Ras Kass' Six Figures.
